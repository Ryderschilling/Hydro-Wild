The founders

WE'RE LINDEY
& CJ.

Like a lot of parents, we kept hitting the same wall in the drink aisle. Every option marketed to our kids came with a catch — sneaky sugar, artificial dyes, a mile-long list of ingredients we couldn't pronounce.

The "healthier" choices were bland enough that no kid would touch them twice. We were tired of choosing between something our kids would actually drink and something we felt good about giving them.

So we decided to build the thing that didn't exist.

Birmingham, AL

What we built

HYDRATION THAT KIDS
ACTUALLY ASK FOR.

HydroWild is a zero-sugar, zero-dye kids hydration drink mix built by parents for parents. Every product delivers a complete vitamin and electrolyte stack — with no artificial dyes, no artificial flavors, and no sugar — sweetened with natural stevia.
